how it works

the best nights start with a saved seat.

01
hosts set the table.

create an intimate table for 4–8 people - a dinner, drinks, brunch, or experience. invite guests from your network. the app handles the rest.

02
guests are hand-picked.

you're only invited if you're a connection or a mutual of someone already at the table. no open seats. no strangers. only people one degree away from someone you trust.

03
connections follow.

after every table, reconnect with the people you met. your circle grows one shared dinner at a time. the best nights leave you with a few new people worth knowing.

save a seat for someone you almost know.

the people one degree away from you are already pre-vetted by someone you trust. hodgepodge makes that invisible connection visible - and turns it into an evening worth remembering.

depth over reach.

no feed. no likes. no algorithm. hodgepodge is a concierge, not a platform. it works quietly in the background so the dinner itself can be the whole thing - no phones, just people.
